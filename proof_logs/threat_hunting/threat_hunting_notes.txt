Threat Hunting Lab Notes:

Queries:
- Identify abnormal login patterns
- Detect lateral movement via SMB/SSH
- Monitor unusual data exfiltration activity

Indicators of Compromise (IOCs):
- Unknown IP addresses
- Suspicious process execution
- Unusual file access patterns

Investigation Steps:
- Collect logs from endpoints and network
- Correlate alerts with SIEM dashboards
- Document findings and escalate confirmed threats
